# Research Methodology

Full agent workflow: **`references/intake-and-search.md`** (requirement intake → multi-dimensional search → reasoning → report).

## Dimension Selection

For each topic, choose **4–6 dimensions** from:

- background and current state
- technical / product architecture
- market or ecosystem comparison
- pricing and business model
- risks and constraints
- future trends

Each dimension needs sub-questions, preferred source types, and falsification criteria (see intake doc).

## Search Standard

- **≥3 search rounds**: landscape → depth → verification
- **≥2 independent sources per dimension**; critical claims **cross-checked**
- **Source tiers**: official/primary (T1) > authoritative secondary (T2) > blogs/forums (T3, verify elsewhere)
- Use WebSearch / codebase / browser — **not model memory alone**
- Log queries and findings in `research-brief.md` before writing `report.md`

## Evidence Rules

- Prefer official or primary sources when possible
- Cross-check important claims with at least 2 independent sources
- Attach date or time range to important facts
- Record support links in `sources.md` **during search**, not after the report
- Mark confidence (high/medium/low) for non-obvious conclusions

## Reasoning Rules

- Document claim → evidence → reasoning chains in `research-brief.md` §6
- Surface source conflicts; do not silently pick one side
- Run a devil's advocate pass before Recommendations
- Log gaps in §7; do not invent facts to fill sections

## Writing Rules

- Complete `research-brief.md` (Phases 0–3) before `report.md`
- Conclusions must be supported by evidence
- Avoid mixing draft opinions into the final conclusion section
- Write `report.md` before building deck or notes

## Minimum Standard

- `research-brief.md` completed for non-trivial topics
- 5+ independent sources unless explicitly relaxed
- explicit references section
- consistent section hierarchy
- no unsupported claims in deck or notes
